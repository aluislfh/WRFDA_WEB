WRFDA TUTORIALS

WRFDA tutorial classes for community users are generally offered once a year in the summer at NCAR Foothills Lab in Boulder, Colorado. The tutorials consist of lectures on the WRF data assimilation system, as well as practical sessions providing hands-on experience with using the software.
